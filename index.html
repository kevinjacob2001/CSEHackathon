About the hackathon

Hackathon

Has the current pandemic got you living the same day over and over again, where you're aware of every moment that goes by? Or did the days since March pass by way too quickly like a montage of blurry memories? Whether you're the former or the latter, we've got you covered.


The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology,

brings you Ingress. A hackathon to challenge you or to get you out of your slump. Come together with like-minded people and attempt to find solutions to myriads of problems and win exciting prizes. For all we know, your solution could potentially make a difference to someone out there.

Why participate?

01

INNOVATION

Create innovative solutions to real -life problems and other social issues. This time-bound event will help collaborate and make a fully functional prototype with viable products which underlines your idea in the best possible way.

02

NETWORKING

You will get an amazing opportunity to confront and connect with people. This will help them to improve your skills and explore new technologies.

03

MASTI

Your participation is really important to us. You will be rewarded with special gifts and goodies. Winners will get what they deserve.
